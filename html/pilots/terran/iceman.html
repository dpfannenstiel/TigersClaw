Name: Michael Casey

Rank: Major

Callsign: Iceman

Tag: Ace of Aces

Major Michael Casey, or Iceman to the crews, ahs racked up more confirmed kills whil serving on the Tiger's Claw than any other pilot in the carrier's history. In the cockpit, he his known for a calm under fire, letter-perfect flying technique, and a deadly aim with ship's weapons, a combination which guarantees disaster for Kilrathi opponents.

Angel, frequent wingman for Iceman, describes flying with him: "You mush learn to listen for Iceman. On the comm unit, in a large engagement, everyone is either shouting or at least very excitable; Iceman is whispering. You have to strain your ears to hear him. It's always ters little statements: 'Moving in.' 'Rolling right.' 'Target in sight.' 'Objectgive accomplished.' 'Head for home.' And I will tell you: He talks ten times as much in the cockpit as he ever talks on the carrier." Iceman is 31, a native of Vancouver, British Columbia.
